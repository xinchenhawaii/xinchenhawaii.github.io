Biography

Hi, I'm Xin Chen, and a Principal Research Scientist at Kuaishou Technology in Palo Alto, CA. I work on the intersection of computer vision, deep learning, edge AI and systemML.

In computer vision and deep learning, I have worked on a variety of areas: including classification, action recognition, model compression and model understanding, and video understanding.

In systemML, I have successfully built three GPU clusters, two, 144 Nvidia Titan Xp GPUs and 128 Nvidia P100 GPUs, which are half price of Nvidia DGX, are for deep learning training, and one, 2 Nvidia S2050 GPUs, is for accelerating CT reconstruction. As a leader of project, I designed and developed a deep learning training framework at Midea Emerging Technology Center (METC). In perspective of algorithm-compiler co-design (memory IO and computation optimization simultaneously), I improved and implemented optimized 3x3 and 1x1 convolutional layers of TNN CPU inference engine on CPU for both mobile and server. These approaches accelerate both dense and sparse conv kernels.

In particular, my recent and ongoing projects consider following applications:

  • Compression-compilation co-design for efficient DNNs inference

  • GPU system co-design with deep learning training

  • Model Understadning
