Xin Chen

I am a Machine Learning Software Engineer at Intel, where I work on computer vision, deep learning, full stack model deployment including model finetuning, prompt optimizaiton, downstream tasks from foundation models, model optimization (Distillation and Pruning), model inference acceleration on GPU and CPU (both kernel-based and compiler-based methods), safety and robustness of generative models evaluation. Before joining Intel, I worked at many academic institutions and industry R&D teams.
