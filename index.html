Biography

I am principal research scientist at Kuaishou Technology in Palo Alto, CA. I am generally interested in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Efficient AI. My long term endeavor is to make AI ubiquitous. In other words, my work enables devices, machines and things to become more intelligent.

Recently, I focus on techniques of co-design and co-develop to obtain efficient DNN models through model compression and novel light modules and compiler techniques to efficiently port DNN models to various platforms including the cloud and the edge.

In particular, my recent and ongoing projects consider following applications:

  • Joint hardware-system-algorithm design and develop efficient models for resource-limited platforms such as mobile, IOT and edge devices

  • Deep Learning compiler techniques (such as TVM, LLVM) for fast, reliable and seamless DNN inference on various platforms such as mobile CPUs and CPUs

  • Computer vision such as classfication, video understadning
